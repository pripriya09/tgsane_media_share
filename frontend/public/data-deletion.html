Data Deletion Instructions

Last Updated: December 26, 2025

How to Request Data Deletion

If you want to delete your data from Auto Post Page application, you have two options:

Option 1: Delete Within the App

  1. Log in to your Auto Post Page account at https://tgsanemedia.netlify.app
  2. Go to your account settings
  3. Click "Disconnect Facebook" to remove all connected Pages and data
  4. Click "Delete Account" to permanently remove your account and all associated data

Option 2: Request Manual Deletion

If you cannot access your account, you can request data deletion by contacting us:

Email: support@yourdomain.com

Subject: Data Deletion Request

Include: Your registered email address or Facebook ID

What Data Will Be Deleted

  • Your account information (username, email)
  • Connected Facebook Page tokens
  • Instagram account connection data
  • Post history and scheduled posts
  • All associated user data

Data Deletion Timeline

Data deletion requests will be processed within 30 days of receiving your request. You will receive a confirmation email once deletion is complete.

Note on Facebook Data

This process only deletes data stored in Auto Post Page. To remove Auto Post Page's access to your Facebook data:
